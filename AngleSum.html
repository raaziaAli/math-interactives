The Proof
Step 1: Draw any triangle. Label the three interior angles A, B, C.
Step 2: Tear off (cut out) each of the three angle corners.
Step 3: Place the three angle pieces side by side, vertices touching a straight line.
Result: The three angles form a perfectly straight line = 180°.
Key Rule
For any triangle — no matter how big, small, or what shape — the three interior angles always add up to exactly 180°. This is true for acute, right, and obtuse triangles.
