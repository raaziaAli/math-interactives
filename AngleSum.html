The Proof
Step 1: Draw any triangle ABC with interior angles A, B, and C.
Step 2: Cut out the three angle wedges from the triangle.
Step 3: Place all three cut-out angles along a straight line.
Conclusion: The three angles fit exactly along the line — forming a straight angle of exactly 180°.
Key Facts
Interior angles The three angles inside any triangle always sum to exactly 180°, no matter the shape or size.
Exterior angle theorem An exterior angle of a triangle equals the sum of the two non-adjacent interior angles.
Types of triangles Acute (all angles < 90°), Right (one angle = 90°), Obtuse (one angle > 90°)
