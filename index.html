Grade 8 Math – Interactive Activity Hub

Choose an activity to explore today. The page is organized into three groups: Geometry & Measurement, Fractions & Percents, and Equations & Scientific Notation. Click on any link to open the interactive page in your browser.

Geometry & Measurement

Explore angle relationships, angle sums, and volume through visual, problem-based tasks.

  • Angle Relationships

    Investigate how angles relate (complementary, supplementary, vertical, etc.) using diagrams and practice problems.

  • Angle Sum in Triangles

    Discover and apply the angle sum of a triangle to find missing angles.

  • Volume Calculator

    Practice finding volume of 3D shapes and connect formulas to real-world contexts.

Fractions, Percents & Ratios

Build conceptual understanding of fractions, percents, and proportional reasoning using food and real-life contexts.

  • Fractions – Conceptual Understanding

    Visual models and tasks to deepen understanding of fractions and their meanings.

  • Percent–Fraction–Decimal

    Practice converting between percents, fractions, and decimals.

  • Percents Practice

    Work with percent increase, decrease, and real-world percentage problems.

  • Pizza Fractions

    Use pizza models to reason about fractions, sharing, and fair division.

  • Meat Lovers Pizza Task

    Apply fraction and percent reasoning in a multi-step pizza problem.

  • Noodle and Cheese Pan

    Solve a ratio/proportion situation using a noodle and cheese pan context.

Equations & Scientific Notation

Translate word problems, solve equations, and work with scientific notation.

  • Translating Words to Equations

    Practice turning real-world situations into algebraic equations.

  • Solving Equations

    Solve one-step and multi-step equations and check your solutions.

  • Simultaneous Equations

    Explore systems of equations and how two lines can intersect, be parallel, or coincide.

  • Multiplying Numbers in Scientific Notation

    Learn how to multiply numbers written in scientific notation efficiently.

  • Scientific Notation Exercise

    Additional practice with operations and comparisons in scientific notation.
