Middle School Math Interactive Practice

Math Activity Hub

Tap a gold button to open an activity. The activities are grouped into Geometry and Measurement, Fractions and Percents, and Equations and Scientific Notation.

Geometry & Measurement

Explore angles and volume through visual, interactive tasks.

  • Angle Relationships

    Practice complementary, supplementary, vertical, and adjacent angles.

  • Angle Sum in Triangles

    Use the angle sum of a triangle to find missing angles.

  • Volume Calculator

    Calculate the volume of 3D shapes and connect math to real objects.

Fractions & Percents

Build strong fraction and percent sense using food and real-life contexts.

  • Fractions – Conceptual Understanding

    Visual models to deepen your understanding of what fractions mean.

  • Percent–Fraction–Decimal

    Practice switching between percents, fractions, and decimals.

  • Percents Practice

    Work with percent increase, decrease, and real-world problems.

  • Pizza Fractions

    Use pizza slices to reason about fractions and fair sharing.

  • Meat Lovers Pizza Task

    Multi-step problem using fractions and percents in a pizza order.

  • Noodle & Cheese Pan

    Solve a ratio/proportion problem in a cooking context.

Equations & Scientific Notation

Connect word problems to equations and work with very large and small numbers.

  • Translating Words to Equations

    Turn real-world situations into algebraic equations.

  • Solving Equations

    Practice solving and checking one-step and multi-step equations.

  • Simultaneous Equations

    Explore systems of equations and their solutions.

  • Multiplying in Scientific Notation

    Learn how to multiply numbers written in scientific notation.

  • Scientific Notation Exercise

    Extra practice comparing and operating with numbers in scientific notation.
